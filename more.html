About this property









This smoke-free hotel features a restaurant, an outdoor pool, and a 24-hour front desk. Free WiFi in public areas and free self parking are also provided. Other amenities include tour/ticket assistance. All 13 rooms offer free WiFi, room service, and flat-screen TVs with cable channels. Other amenities available to guests include electric kettles, free bottled water, and showers. Vivere Azure offers 13 air-conditioned accommodations with complimentary bottled water and slippers. Flat-screen televisions come with cable channels. Bathrooms include showers and complimentary toiletries. Guests can surf the web using the complimentary wireless Internet access. Housekeeping is provided daily.


How to get there







By private vehicle, Take the SLEX and STAR Tollway until you reach the Diversion Road. Under the flyover, turn right towards Bauan and continue until you reach Anilao. From there, turn left in the direction of San Teodoro and drive straight until you reach Vivere Azure on your right.

By public transport, From Cubao, take a bus bound for Batangas City Public Terminal. Take a jeepney to Mabini, Batangas, and then alight at Mabini Crossing. You may then ride a tricycle going to Vivere Azure.
